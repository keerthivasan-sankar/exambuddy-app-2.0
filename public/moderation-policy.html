Content Moderation Policy

1. Overview

ExamBuddy is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and authentic environment for all users. This Content Moderation Policy outlines the standards and procedures we use to manage content and behavior on our platform.

2. Prohibited Content

Users are strictly prohibited from posting, sharing, or transmitting any content that falls into the following categories:

  • Spam: Unsolicited promotional content, repetitive messages, or automated bot activity.
  • Plagiarism: Content that infringes on the intellectual property rights of others.
  • Obscene or Explicit Content: Nudity, sexually explicit material, or excessively violent content.
  • Hate Speech and Harassment: Content that attacks, threatens, or insults individuals based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sex, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or medical condition.
  • Illegal Activities: Content promoting or facilitating illegal acts, including the sale of prohibited goods.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app using the "Report" feature available on profiles and chats. We encourage our community to actively participate in maintaining a safe environment.

4. Enforcement Actions

Upon receiving a report or detecting a violation, our moderation team will review the content. Depending on the severity of the violation, we may take the following actions:

  • Remove the violating content immediately.
  • Issue a warning to the user.
  • Temporarily suspend the user's account.
  • Permanently ban the user from the platform.

We reserve the right to report severe violations, such as threats of violence or child exploitation, to the appropriate law enforcement authorities.
